Why Bhuratna?

As we grow in our consciousness we realize the deep connection we have with our surroundings. With surroundings, I mean to say the space that holds us, The Earth on which we walk, The water that nourishes the life within and around, fire - the powerhouse of life on this planet and the ether which plays an important role but which is beyond the perception and understanding of humans unless strived for it. We realize that the deterioration of this ecosystem will inevitably affect the life and health of all the dwellers of this planet. An enriched, nourished mother earth will pass all her goodness and well-being to her offspring With this understanding, millets are the best choice that have the potency to restore the ecological balance and provide nourishment to us which is missing in our previaling diet. A fiber-rich grain packed with many essential nutrients can be a cheap and easily accessible solution to many chronic and lifestyle disorders.

So why not embrace it? Our simple step of making the right choice and a little alteration in our diet may have a significant impact on our health, the economy of our country, and the ecology at large.

Let's contribute in our little ways towards the betterment and enrichment of life
